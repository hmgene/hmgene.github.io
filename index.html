Greetings.

Hello, I am a crypto-bioinformaticist specializing in the study of information within living organisms. Leveraging this knowledge, we can undertake various endeavors, such as drug development, cancer diagnosis, and comprehension of biological mechanisms. My current focus lies in transforming bioinformatics into tangible value without reliance on volatile financial resources. This underscores a crucial point: numerous talented bioinformaticians face setbacks not due to lack of skill, but rather due to insufficient support.

My research topics have been advancing toward integration of RNA processs combining alternative splicing and polyadenylation in coperation with co-transcriptional regulation, where RNA polymerase II plays important role.
