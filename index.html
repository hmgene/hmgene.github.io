Greetings.

Hello, I am a bioinformaticist studying evolution of algorithms living in organisms. Many biological processes including transcription of DNA and translation of RNA are algorithms. In other words those problems are soluble by computation. Thesedays, advanced next/third-generation sequencing technology allows us to store the whole DNA/RNA/protein bioinformation in time and space dimensions in electric repositories. My research topics focuses on human RNA processing including alternative splicing and polyadenylation in coperation with co-transcriptional regulation, where RNA polymerase II plays important role. All of my algorithms are implemented in HMTools
